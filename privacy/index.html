Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you book a ride, create an account, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and location data required for pick-up and drop-off.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to facilitate your transportation requests, process payments, and provide you with support. We also use data to improve our services and ensure the safety of our passengers and drivers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information with drivers to complete your requested ride and with service providers who assist us in operating our platform.

4. Your Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can also opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@cityride24h.com.
